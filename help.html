Find a new ending for your verb!

Use this website to conjugate Spanish verbs in the present tense.

  1. VERB TYPE? Is the verb is an -ar, -er, or -ir verb?
  2. HOW MANY PEOPLE? Are you describing only one person or multiple people?
  3. WHO? Which subject pronoun matches who or what you are describing?
  4. ROOT+ENDING: The website tells you what ending would replace the "ar", "er", or "ir" part of the verb.

    Remember that you need to place the root of the word before the new ending!

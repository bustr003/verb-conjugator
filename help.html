Find a new ending for your verb!

Uset this website to conjugate Spanish verbs in the present tense.

  • First, the website asks you if the verb is an -ar, -er, or -ir verb.
  • Second, the website asks if you are describing only one person or describing multiple people.
  • Third, the website asks for the subject pronoun that matches who or what you are describing.
  • Fourth, the website tells you what ending would replace the "ar", "er", or "ir" part of the verb.

    Remember that you need to place the root of the word before the new ending!

Find a new ending for your verb!

I created this website in 2021 by to help Spanish learners figure out the new ending for a verb based on the subject pronoun.

The inspiration for this website was to help my sister practice going through steps to conjugate verbs. I noticed that during her Spanish homework, my sister had a tough time while trying to use hand-drawn charts on pieces of paper to figure out what ending she should use for a word... it also seemed hard to figure out WHY this process was being done.

As a person who is passionate about helping people learn (and have fun while learning), I wanted to try making the process more simple, step-by-step, fun, and interactive.

I made sure to consider my sister's favorite colors when choosing the color palette for this website, and I also wanted to make sure that there is never much information on the page at one time. I chose to add fun images to enhance visual learning, repetition, and positive feedback.
